Personal • Jul 29, 2018

Startup Leadership, Business

Business/Entrepreneurship

All-In with Chamath, Jason, Sacks & Friedberg
Host(s): Chamath Palihapitiya, David Sacks, David Friedberg | Topic: Economics, Technology, Politics, Social, Poker
Description: Industry veterans, degenerate gamblers & besties Chamath Palihapitiya, Jason Calacanis, David Sacks & David Friedberg cover all things economic, tech, political, social & poker.

Masters of Scale
Host(s): Reid Hoffman | Topic: Startup Leadership
Description: An original podcast hosted by LinkedIn Co-Founder and Greylock Partner Reid Hoffman, showcasing how companies grow from zero to a gazillion. The series unfolds like a music-infused detective story as Hoffman tests his theories with famous founders. Masters of Scale is the first American media program to commit to a 50-50 gender balance for guests.

How I Built This with Guy Raz
Host(s): Guy Raz | Topic: Business
Description: A podcast about innovators, entrepreneurs, idealists, and the stories behind the movements they built.

Internet History Podcast
Host(s): Brian McCullough | Topic: Internet History, Business Stories/Lessons
Description: An interactive history project producing a book on the history of the internet era by crowdsourcing facts and first-person accounts from listeners. Each podcast represents a new chapter focusing on some section of internet history.
